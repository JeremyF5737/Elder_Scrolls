<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><p>There are many <i><b>books</b></i> in <a href="/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind" title="Morrowind:Morrowind">Morrowind</a>, attempting to provide players with an authentic experience by letting them delve as deeply as they want into the lore of <a href="/wiki/Lore:Tamriel" title="Lore:Tamriel">Tamriel</a>. Acquiring and reading them all in-game is a difficult task, so they are collected here. On this page there are further book categories as well as a list of all the books that appear in the game. A list of books broken down by subject can be found <a href="/wiki/Morrowind:Books_by_Subject" title="Morrowind:Books by Subject">here</a>.</p>
<p><b>Special Categories of Books</b></p>
<dl>
<dt><a href="/wiki/Morrowind:Faction_Books" title="Morrowind:Faction Books">Faction Books</a></dt>
<dd>Most factions in the game have books that document what they believe.</dd>

<dd>Shorter notes, ledgers, logs, etc.</dd>
</dl>
